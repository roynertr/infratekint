**COBie** turns scattered project information into a structured, exchangeable format for owners and operators.

## How we help

- **Requirements** — define what data is needed at handover and who owns it.
- **Mapping** — align model properties and spreadsheets to COBie expectations.
- **Validation** — checks before handover so O&M teams receive usable data.
- **Training** — so your staff can sustain the workflow after go-live.

We focus on practical deliverables your facilities team can adopt—not theory without execution.
