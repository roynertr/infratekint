**Client:** EpsaLabco

**Clash Management:**

- **Collision Matrix Creation:** Mapped potential interferences for preemptive resolution.

- **Multidisciplinary Coordination:** Harmonized architectural, structural, plumbing, mechanical, and electrical systems.

- **Issue Assignment:** Delegated resolution tasks across project cycles.

- **Reporting:** Compiled comprehensive reports and assembled the final federated model.

**Outcomes:**

- Identified over 3,500 clash instances.

- Detected more than 950 critical potential field interferences.

- Estimated savings: RD$8 million.
