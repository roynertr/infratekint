**Client:** New York Management & Construction Corp

**Implementation Scope:**

- **Workflow Protocol Design:** Customized strategies to streamline BIM processes.

- **Virtual Reality Integration:** Interactive VR experiences within BIM models.

- **IFC Model Interactivity:** Engaging with IFC models for enhanced project collaboration.

- **IFC Model Quality Control:** Ensuring the precision and accuracy of IFC model data.

- **4D Simulation Creation:** Visual project planning and timeline simulation.

- **Quantity Takeoffs:** Utilizing Cubit and Bluebeam Revu for accurate material and cost estimation.
