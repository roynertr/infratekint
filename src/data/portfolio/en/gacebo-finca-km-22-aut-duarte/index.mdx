**Project Features:**

- **BIM Architectural Modeling:** Crafted a detailed architectural BIM model for the gazebo.

- **BIM Structural Modeling:** Developed a structural BIM framework to ensure stability and design integrity.

- **Basic Collision Detection:** Implemented clash detection with furnishings to streamline design.

- **Construction Planning and Oversight:** Managed and supervised the project from inception to completion.

- **Built Area:** 70 sqm (approximately 753 sqft).
