**Reality capture** closes the gap between what was designed and what exists on site.

## Services

- **Laser scanning and point clouds** to document existing conditions with precision.
- **As-built documentation** that reflects the current state of buildings and industrial assets.
- **Scan-to-BIM workflows** for renovation, retrofit, and coordination decisions.
- **Site verification** that compares captured reality with design or construction models.
- **Workflow-ready deliverables** in formats your team can use downstream.

We align capture scope, accuracy, and format with how you will use the data downstream.
