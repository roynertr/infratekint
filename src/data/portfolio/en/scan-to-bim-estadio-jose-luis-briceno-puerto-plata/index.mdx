## Overview

In February 2023, INFRATEK delivered a Scan-to-BIM modeling project for the José Luis Briceño Baseball Stadium located in Puerto Plata, Dominican Republic. The project was developed from client-provided point cloud data to support digital documentation, existing-condition modeling, and BIM-based asset visualization.

The scope included the interpretation and processing of laser scan information to generate a coordinated BIM model representing the stadium's architectural and structural conditions. The model was developed to support digital coordination workflows and interoperable data exchange standards.

## Deliverables

Final deliverables included:

- Native Autodesk Revit model
- IFC4 export for Open BIM interoperability
- DWG documentation files
- Supporting model deliverables for coordination and visualization purposes
