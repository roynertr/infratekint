_Last updated February 2026_

INFRATEK (“INFRATEK,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share information when you visit our website at **infratekint.com** (the “Website”).

**This policy is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice. Consult qualified counsel for advice specific to your situation.**

## Who we are

INFRATEK provides BIM, digital twin, COBie, reality capture, and related technology services with operations touching the **United States** and **Dominican Republic**.

**Contact:** [info@infratekint.com](mailto:info@infratekint.com)

## Information we collect

### Information you give us

- **Contact form:** If you submit our contact form, we collect your name, email address, optional phone number, and message content.

### Automatically collected information

Like most websites, our hosting infrastructure may automatically collect technical data such as IP address, browser type, approximate location derived from IP, referrer, pages viewed, and timestamps. The exact data depends on our hosting provider’s logs and configuration.

## How we use your information

We use the information described above to:

- Respond to your inquiries sent through the contact form.
- Operate, secure, improve, and analyze use of the Website.
- Remember your language preference (see Cookies below).

We do **not** sell your personal information.

## Cookies and similar technologies

We use a limited cookie to remember your **language choice** (`infratek_locale`) when you switch between English and Spanish. This cookie is set with Path `/`, a one-year lifetime, `SameSite=Lax`, and `Secure` when the site is served over HTTPS.

If you block cookies, language preference may not persist between visits.

## Email delivery (Resend)

When you submit the contact form, we transmit your message using **Resend** (email delivery infrastructure). Resend receives the fields needed to deliver mail (such as recipient, sender metadata, reply-to address, subject, and message body) in accordance with [Resend’s policies](https://resend.com/legal). Please review Resend’s documentation and privacy notices for details on how they process data.

## Legal bases (where applicable)

Depending on where you are located, we may rely on one or more of the following bases: performance of a request you initiated (responding to contact messages), legitimate interests (operating and securing the Website, understanding aggregate use), or consent where required (for example for certain cookies or communications where mandated by law).

## Retention

We retain contact submissions and related correspondence only as long as needed to fulfill your request, maintain business records consistent with ordinary practice, and comply with applicable law. Aggregated technical logs may be retained in line with hosting provider retention settings.

## Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to certain processing, or port your personal information, and/or to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise rights that apply to you, email **info@infratekint.com**. We may need to verify your request where permitted by law.

## Children

The Website is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

## International transfers

We may process information in the United States, Dominican Republic, and other locations where our service providers operate. Where required by law, appropriate safeguards may apply.

## Third-party links

The Website may link to sites we do not control. Those sites have their own privacy policies; we encourage you to read them before providing information.

## Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date reflects the latest revision. Continued use of the Website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy where permitted by applicable law.
