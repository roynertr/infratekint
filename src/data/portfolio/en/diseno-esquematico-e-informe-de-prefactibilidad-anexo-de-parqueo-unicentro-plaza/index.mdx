**Project Overview:**

- **Drone Survey of Existing Conditions:** Conducted aerial surveys to capture detailed exterior site conditions.

- **Point Cloud Creation via Aerial Photogrammetry:** Produced a detailed point cloud for precise modeling.

- **Parking Levels Modeling:** Developed architectural models for the parking structure levels.

- **Cost Estimation:** Provided detailed cost assessments for budget planning.

- **Time and Methodology Analysis:** Analyzed project timelines and work methodologies to optimize construction plans.
