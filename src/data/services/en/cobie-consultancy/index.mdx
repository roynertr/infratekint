**COBie** turns scattered project information into a structured, exchangeable format for owners and operators.

- **Data validation and quality checks** so handover information is complete, consistent, and usable.
- **COBie-based O&M manuals** that connect structured data with practical operations needs.
- **Facility handover workflows** that clarify responsibilities, formats, and acceptance criteria.

We focus on practical deliverables your facilities team can adopt, not theory without execution.
