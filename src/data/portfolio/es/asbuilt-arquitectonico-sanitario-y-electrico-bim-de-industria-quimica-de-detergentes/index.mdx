**Client:** Productos Químicos Panamericanos

**Project Details:**

- **Interior & Exterior Survey:** Conducted comprehensive site surveys for detailed modeling.

- **Architectural BIM As-Built:** Developed an accurate As-Built BIM model capturing the site’s architectural features.

- **Plumbing BIM As-Built:** Crafted a precise As-Built BIM model of the sanitary systems.

- **Electrical BIM As-Built:** Formulated a detailed As-Built BIM model of the electrical installations.

- **Permitting Plan Drafting:** Created formal plans to facilitate municipal permit applications.

- **Modeled Area:** 2,500 sqm (approximately 26,910 sqft).
