**Reality capture** closes the gap between what was designed and what exists on site.

## Services

- **As-built documentation** — point clouds and models that reflect current conditions.
- **Verification** — compare scan to model to catch deviations early.
- **Renovation & retrofit** — a reliable base for design decisions in existing buildings.
- **Workflow fit** — deliverables your team can consume in the tools you already use.

We align capture scope, accuracy, and format with how you will use the data downstream.
