Commissioned for precise, up-to-date documentation and geometric details, this project showcases our expertise in creating LOD 200 BIM models for design, planning, and construction phases in the industrial sector. Utilizing 3D laser scanning technology, we delivered a comprehensive model with the following specifications:

- **Project Scope:** BIM Modeling for 30,000 sqm Industrial Rehabilitation

- **Level of Detail:** Delivered at LOD 200 for enhanced design and planning accuracy

- **Modeling Disciplines:** Detailed Architectural and Structural BIM components

- **Project Deliverables:** Native .RVT Model, IFC 2X3 Coordination View, and Geo-Referenced CAD Drawings for optimal project coordination

- **Location Highlight:** Punta Cana – A strategic hub for industrial developments

- **Industry Focus:** Custom BIM solutions tailored for the Tobacco Industry

Our client, SERCITEC, benefited from a digitally transformed space, ready for efficient rehabilitation and operation.
