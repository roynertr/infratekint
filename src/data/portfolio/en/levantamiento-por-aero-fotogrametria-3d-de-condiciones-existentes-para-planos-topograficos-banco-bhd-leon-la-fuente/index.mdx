**Photogrammetry Survey:**

- **3D BIM Photogrammetry:** Generated precise 3D BIM imagery for site analysis.

- **3D Mass Modeling:** Created a comprehensive 3D mass model of the environment.

- **Aerial Photographic Record:** Captured an extensive aerial photo archive of the site.
