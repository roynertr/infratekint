INFRATEK delivers **end-to-end BIM services** so owners, designers, and contractors can work from coordinated, trustworthy, and usable project information. We help teams implement BIM in a practical way: from standards, roles, and workflows to coordination, validation, and model delivery.

## BIM Implementation

We implement BIM as a working system, not as a collection of models. We help define objectives, standards, responsibilities, coordination workflows, quality criteria, and deliverables so the project team can sustain the process throughout delivery.

### What it includes

- BIM maturity assessment and project needs review.
- Definition of BIM objectives and priority BIM uses.
- BIM Execution Plan / BEP development or review.
- Roles, responsibilities, and deliverables matrix.
- Modeling standards, naming conventions, parameters, and information structure.
- CDE setup or alignment based on the project workflow.
- Coordination, review, issue management, and quality control workflows.
- Practical training for internal and external teams.
- Adoption support to keep BIM from becoming a theoretical requirement.

## What we deliver

### BIM Implementation

We standardize how the project creates, reviews, and delivers BIM information. This may include BEP development, modeling standards, coordination workflows, CDE setup, responsibility matrices, and training.

### BIM Coordination

We help detect, organize, and resolve conflicts before they become field issues, using clear review, tracking, and issue-closeout workflows.

### BIM Management

We align teams, models, deliverables, and schedules so the BIM workflow stays connected to the project's real delivery needs.

### BIM Quality Control

We review models, parameters, information structure, standards compliance, and deliverable consistency to reduce errors and rework.

### 3D and As-Built Modeling

We develop BIM models for design, coordination, documentation, and existing conditions, including Scan to BIM support when applicable.

### 5D BIM and Quantity Takeoff

We connect models with quantities, measurements, and cost-related data to support estimating, control, and decision-making.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can INFRATEK help implement BIM from scratch?

Yes. We can support the process from initial assessment to standards, BEP development, roles, coordination workflows, quality control, and team training.

### What is the difference between BIM implementation and ISO 19650 implementation?

BIM implementation defines how the team uses models, data, and workflows on the project. ISO 19650 provides an international framework for information management. We can apply both depending on the client's maturity and needs.

### Do you work with owners, designers, or contractors?

Yes. We adapt the scope depending on the client: owners needing information requirements, designers needing coordination and quality, or contractors needing buildable models.

### Can you review existing BIM models?

Yes. We can audit models, parameters, naming conventions, information structure, standards compliance, coordination, and handover readiness.

### Can you support BEP development?

Yes. We can develop, review, or improve a BIM Execution Plan so it is practical, clear, and aligned with project objectives.
